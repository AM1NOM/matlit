MERCIA ROBOTICS

Mercia Robotics is the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) team from Mercia School. Our students learn engineering, coding, and teamwork by designing competitive robots. We compete in the annual FTC challenge and also run outreach sessions to inspire younger students.
